Install the LP extension

Adds a one-click Scrape this page button to your TikTok Shop pages, so the numbers you're looking at land in LP-OS instead of being copied by hand. Takes about two minutes to set up. Chrome only.

1 · Download

Download lp-os-extension.zip, then unzip it. You'll get one folder named lp-os-extension.

Keep that folder somewhere permanent — your Documents, say. Chrome runs the extension from wherever you leave it, so moving or deleting the folder later removes the extension.

2 · Add it to Chrome

  1. Type chrome://extensions into the address bar and press Enter.
  2. Switch on Developer mode — the toggle in the top-right corner.
  3. Click Load unpacked, then choose the lp-os-extension folder you just unzipped.
  4. Click the puzzle-piece icon in Chrome's toolbar and pin LP-OS TikTok Shop Scraper so it's always one click away.

Developer mode is just how Chrome installs an extension that isn't in its store. Chrome will show a reminder banner about it on startup — that's expected, and you can dismiss it.

3 · Tell it who you are

  1. Click the LP extension icon in your toolbar.
  2. Enter your LP-OS username — your handle, like @boosteddealsdaily — and click Set.

Everything you scrape is credited to that name, so it only needs doing once per computer.

4 · Use it

Open any of your TikTok Shop pages — Partner Center, the LIVE dashboard, Streamer Compass, or your order pages — then click the LP icon and hit Scrape this page.

The popup tells you whether the page you're on is one it can read. If the button is greyed out, you're on a page it doesn't cover — navigate to the dashboard or order you want and try again.

Something not working? Confirm you're signed in to TikTok Shop in the same Chrome profile, then reload the page and try once more.
